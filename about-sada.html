About SADA

SADA COIN is the native utility token powering the BridgeX protocol — a decentralized bridge that enables fast, secure, and trustless interoperability between the Solana and Cardano blockchains.

Designed to solve the lack of cross-chain infrastructure between these two major networks, SADA COIN is used for transaction fees, staking, governance, and incentivizing participants within the BridgeX ecosystem.

With its advanced architecture leveraging Solana smart contracts and Cardano's Plutus scripts, BridgeX facilitates seamless token transfers, cross-chain dApp development, and decentralized applications that span both ecosystems.

SADA COIN represents the backbone of this vision — a truly interoperable future for DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and beyond.
